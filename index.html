01 — The Brand

MONSTERS, MISFITS, AND FORGOTTEN THINGS COME ALIVE AFTER MIDNIGHT

Nocturnal Chillers is where the night comes alive — an ever-expanding universe of heroes, monsters, misfits, and other moonlit surprises springing forth from our cinematic AI video stories. We also serve up a mix of memes, graphics, bite-sized video clips, and even written content that ranges in style from horror to retro-nostalgia to humor, sometimes a fusion of all three! From lurking creatures to unforgettable legends, Chillers celebrates the weird, the terrifying, and the mischievous side of storytelling.

If you come alive after midnight, welcome home.

Nocturnal Chillers is a storytelling universe that produces short cinematic AI horror stories blending dark humor, nostalgia, and unsettling mythology. Each story introduces characters with strange pasts, emotional depth, and memorable personalities.

These characters live across short-form videos, expanding lore, and wearable culture. Horror as humanity — where monsters are metaphors for real emotional experiences. The brand doesn't chase trends. It builds a world and invites you inside.
